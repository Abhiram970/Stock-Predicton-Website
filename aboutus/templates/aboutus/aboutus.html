Our Mission


We aim to provide our users the best predictions possible for the stocks of their company. Machine learning is effectively implemented in forecasting stock prices. The objective is to predict the stock prices in order to make more informed and accurate investment decisions. We propose a stock price prediction system that integrates mathematical functions, machine learning, and other external factors for the purpose of achieving better stock prediction accuracy and issuing profitable trades. Stock market trends forecast is one of the most current topics and a significant research challenge due to its dynamic and unstable nature. Therefore, to help the companies make efficient trading decisions, we present to them this website that will help them view the trend of their stocks in future and thus help them hold a firm grip in the stock market.


About Us

We are a team of four people pursuing a bachelor's degree in CSE with specialization in AI and ML at VIT Chennai. Having a desire to help the society, we have tried to make a website that will help the corporate companies predict their investment returns for the given stocks. We have tried to develop a machine learning model which is deployed as the backend of the website. The predictions are made with help of this ML model which can then be used by the user to plan their business strategies and compete in the contemporary business world.
